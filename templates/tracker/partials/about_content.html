About Open Science Tracker

Promoting transparency and reproducibility in scientific research through comprehensive tracking and analysis of open science practices.

Our Mission

Open Science Tracker is dedicated to advancing transparency in scientific research by systematically tracking and analyzing open science practices across academic publications.

We believe that transparent research practices—including open data sharing, code availability, conflict of interest disclosure, funding transparency, and protocol registration—are fundamental to the integrity and reproducibility of scientific knowledge.

Comprehensive Database

Track transparency indicators across thousands of research papers from multiple scientific journals and databases.

Real-time Analytics

Monitor trends in open science practices across different research fields and publication venues.

Advanced Search

Find papers based on transparency criteria, research fields, journals, and publication characteristics.

Open API

Access transparency data programmatically through our comprehensive REST API for researchers and developers.
