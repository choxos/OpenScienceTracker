About OST

Advancing transparency and reproducibility in scientific research through comprehensive tracking and assessment of open science practices.

What is the Open Science Tracker?

The Open Science Tracker (OST) is a comprehensive research transparency assessment platform that analyzes and tracks open science practices across scientific publications. Our mission is to promote transparency, reproducibility, and accessibility in research by providing detailed metrics and insights into how well the scientific community is adopting open science principles.

OST systematically evaluates multiple transparency indicators in published research papers, including data sharing, code availability, conflict of interest disclosure, funding transparency, protocol registration, and open access publication. By aggregating these metrics across journals, research fields, and time periods, we provide valuable insights into the current state and trends of research transparency.

Our Methodology

Data Collection

OST utilizes automated text mining and analysis techniques to systematically assess transparency indicators across scientific publications. Our data collection process involves:

  • Automated scanning of research publications
  • Natural language processing for transparency indicators
  • Integration with multiple scientific databases
  • Continuous monitoring and updates
